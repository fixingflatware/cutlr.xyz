# 🍴 CUTLR
### We're cutlers. We fix bent flatware.

---

### The tools you use every day have become twisted.

Simple software is getting locked behind heavy paywalls. Useful products are
bloated, overengineered, and weirdly expensive. It feels like someone took
perfectly straight utensils and bent them — just to charge you a premium to
use them.

We prefer things straight.

CUTLR is a growing suite of lightweight, un-twisted alternatives. No bloat,
no venture capital to feed. Just the core features, at what they should
actually cost. Everything is open source under the **MIT License** and
natively supports **MCP (Model Context Protocol)** — bring your own AI,
and it can use every CUTLR tool out of the box.

One account, every tool. Sign in once at cutlr.xyz and you're in everywhere.

---

## 🛠️ The Suite (so far)

**📝 notes.cutlr.xyz**
A clean, minimal canvas for your thoughts. Fully accessible via prompt
through native MCP integration.

**📋 tasks.cutlr.xyz**
A straight to-do list. Add, check, done. Tell your AI "put it on my list"
and it's there.

**⏱️ track.cutlr.xyz**
Time tracking without the timesheet theater. Start a timer, stop it, see
where your hours went. Or just tell your agent to do it for you.

**📅 polls.cutlr.xyz**
Pick a few dates, share the link, find out when everyone's free. Or let
your AI coordinate the perfect slot for you.

**🎟️ rsvp.cutlr.xyz**
An event page and a guest list. No ticketing fees, no platform tax on
getting people in a room.

**🔖 bookmarks.cutlr.xyz**
Save a link, tag it, find it again. Your reading list, not an algorithm's.

*New tools ship regularly. The suite keeps growing.*
