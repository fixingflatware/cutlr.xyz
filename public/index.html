// FAQ

Switching, without the headache.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Every tool is just a website — open a link, sign in once, and you're working. On any device, nothing to download.

Where is my data hosted?

On secure European (EU) infrastructure — GDPR-compliant and private, protected under EU data-protection law.

Can I bring my stuff over from other apps?

Yes. Import your notes, links and contacts via standard formats (CSV, Markdown, JSON), and export them back out whenever you like. Your data never gets held hostage.

Is it really cancel-anytime?

Really. It's month-to-month — no annual prepay, no "contact sales", no cancellation maze. Stop paying and access simply ends when the month you paid for runs out.

What happens to my data if I leave?

You export it in standard formats and it's yours. Or run the open-source version yourself and take the whole thing with you — same tools, on your own machine, free.

Why is it so cheap? What's the catch?

No catch. No investors to repay, no bloat to maintain, no sales team to fund — so the price is just what the tools should cost. €2.99/mo for everything, or free if you self-host.
